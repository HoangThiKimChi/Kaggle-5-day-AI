## 1. Phân loại theo chức năng

### 🔢 Sequencing — Sắp xếp ý theo thứ tự
| Từ/Cụm | Ví dụ |
|---|---|
| Firstly, | Firstly, robots can work faster than humans. |
| Secondly, | Secondly, they do not need to take breaks. |
| Thirdly, | Thirdly, they can perform dangerous tasks. |
| Finally, | Finally, the cost of maintenance is high. |
| To begin with, | To begin with, there are clear economic benefits. |
| Last but not least, | Last but not least, safety must be considered. |

### ➕ Adding — Thêm ý bổ sung
| Từ/Cụm | Ví dụ |
|---|---|
| Furthermore, | Furthermore, students who exercise perform better academically. |
| Moreover, | Moreover, the environmental impact cannot be ignored. |
| In addition, | In addition, the government should provide clear guidelines. |
| In addition to this, | In addition to this, gyms are often part of larger chains. |
| What is more, | What is more, the policy would be difficult to enforce. |
| Also, | It is convenient and also cost-effective. |
| Not only that, but | Not only that, but the sport also improves mental focus. |
| not only … but also … | It not only saves time but also reduces costs. |

### 🔄 Contrasting — Tương phản / đối lập
| Từ/Cụm | Ví dụ |
|---|---|
| However, | However, there are also significant drawbacks. |
| On the other hand, | On the other hand, small shops give character to a town. |
| Nevertheless, | Nevertheless, the benefits outweigh the risks. |
| Nonetheless, | Nonetheless, the policy has some merit. |
| Despite this, | Despite this, many people continue to drive to work. |
| Despite + noun, | Despite the advantages, the system is expensive. |
| Although + clause, | Although recycling is important, it is not enough alone. |
| While + clause, | While some support the idea, others strongly oppose it. |
| Whereas + clause, | Whereas cities offer more jobs, rural areas offer a better quality of life. |
| Even though + clause, | Even though it would be difficult to manage, it would encourage recycling. |
| In spite of + noun, | In spite of the cost, many countries have adopted this system. |
| Yet, | Yet, the evidence suggests otherwise. |

> ⚠️ **Error warning (Pauline Cullen):** *because of*, *in spite of*, *despite* phải đi với NOUN, không phải clause.
> ✅ *despite the bad weather* — ❌ *despite the weather was bad*

### 📝 Giving Examples — Đưa ví dụ
| Từ/Cụm | Ví dụ |
|---|---|
| For example, | For example, in Vietnam, millions of students study online. |
| For instance, | For instance, music shops employ knowledgeable staff. |
| Such as | Activities such as cycling and swimming improve fitness. |
| To illustrate this, | To illustrate this, consider the case of Singapore. |
| By way of example, | By way of example, my city has over 200 gyms. |

### 💡 Giving Reasons / Cause & Effect
| Từ/Cụm | Ví dụ |
|---|---|
| because | People recycle because it reduces waste. |
| because of + noun | Because of the cost, many cannot afford private healthcare. |
| as a result, | As a result, unemployment may increase significantly. |
| consequently, | Consequently, the government must act quickly. |
| therefore, | Therefore, education is the most effective solution. |
| This means that | This means that students have more flexibility. |
| This leads to | This leads to a reduction in carbon emissions. |
| as | People feel stressed as they have too much work. |

### 🎯 Giving an Opinion
| Từ/Cụm | Dùng khi |
|---|---|
| In my opinion, | Nêu quan điểm cá nhân |
| In my view, | Nêu quan điểm cá nhân |
| Personally, | Nhấn mạnh đây là ý kiến cá nhân |
| I believe that | Nêu quan điểm |
| I strongly believe that | Nhấn mạnh quan điểm |
| I tend to agree / disagree | Đồng ý / không đồng ý một phần |
| It seems to me that | Nêu quan điểm nhẹ hơn |
| My own view is that | Nêu quan điểm |
| I would argue that | Đưa ra lập luận |
| From my perspective, | Từ góc nhìn cá nhân |
| All things considered, | Kết luận sau khi cân nhắc |

### 🔚 Concluding
| Từ/Cụm | Ví dụ |
|---|---|
| In conclusion, | In conclusion, there are clear advantages and disadvantages. |
| To sum up, | To sum up, the benefits of technology outweigh the drawbacks. |
| To summarise, | To summarise, both sides have valid arguments. |
| In summary, | In summary, weighing up both sides, I would say... |
| Overall, | Overall, I believe the advantages outweigh the disadvantages. |
| Weighing up both sides, | Weighing up both sides, I lean towards agreement. |

### 🔀 Alternative / Other Perspectives
| Từ/Cụm | Ví dụ |
|---|---|
| alternatively, | Alternatively, the government could offer tax incentives. |
| on the one hand … on the other hand | On the one hand, it saves time. On the other hand, it is costly. |
| similarly, | Similarly, other European countries have adopted this approach. |
| in other words, | In other words, the policy needs more careful planning. |

## 2. Opinion Adverbs (Pauline Cullen — Unit 24)

> Dùng ở đầu câu, **luôn có dấu phẩy** sau: *Personally,* — *Clearly,* — *Obviously,*

| Adverb | Nghĩa |
|---|---|
| Personally, | this is my own opinion |
| Clearly, / Obviously, | it is easy to understand |
| Justifiably, | it is well deserved / correct |
| Fortunately, / Thankfully, | I believe this is a good thing |
| Unfortunately, | I believe this is a sad thing |

## 3. Synonyms cho Good/Bad Points (Mindset L1/L3)

| Advantages | Disadvantages |
|---|---|
| advantages | disadvantages |
| benefits | drawbacks |
| positive aspects | negative aspects |
| pros | cons |
| upsides / on the plus side | downsides / on the downside |
| good points | bad points |
| merits | issues / problems |

## 4. Lỗi thường gặp với linking words (Mindset L3)

- ❌ **Overusing**: không phải câu nào cũng cần discourse marker — nếu ý đã rõ, không cần thêm
- ❌ **Misusing**: *because* (conjunction) ≠ *because of* (preposition)
- ✅ Dùng đúng: sequence → addition → contrast → example → conclusion
- ✅ Phải có dấu phẩy sau các từ: *Firstly,* *However,* *In addition,* *For example,* *In conclusion,*
- ❌ KHÔNG viết: *In conclusion, to sum up,* — chỉ chọn 1 cách mở đầu kết luận
