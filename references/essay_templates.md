# Essay Templates — Mẫu bài viết theo từng dạng đề
> Tổng hợp từ: Collins A2+, Mindset L1/L2/L3, High-scoring Model Answers
> Dùng cho: `guide_essay_section` tool — hướng dẫn từng phần intro/body/conclusion

---

## Template 1: Opinion Essay (Agree/Disagree)

### Cấu trúc tổng thể:
```
[Introduction]
[Body 1 — Reason 1]
[Body 2 — Reason 2]
[Conclusion]
```

### Introduction template:
```
[Paraphrase đề bằng cách thay từ/cấu trúc].
[Thesis: I strongly agree/disagree with this view for two main reasons.]
```

**Ví dụ (đề: "Music brings different cultures together"):**
> "It has been argued that music serves as a universal language, capable of uniting people from vastly different cultural backgrounds. I strongly agree with this view, and in this essay I will explain two key reasons why I believe music plays this important role."

---

### Body 1 template:
```
[Topic sentence: The first/main reason why I [agree/disagree] is that [idea].]
[Explain: This is because [explanation].]
[Develop: [Cause-effect or additional detail].]
[Example: For example/For instance, [specific example from real life].]
```

**Ví dụ:**
> "The first reason why I agree is that music creates shared emotional experiences that transcend cultural and linguistic barriers. When people listen to the same piece of music, they feel the same emotions regardless of their nationality or native language. This common emotional response helps break down cultural prejudices and fosters a sense of connection. For example, the global popularity of K-pop has united fans from Vietnam, Brazil, and Senegal who share little else in common."

---

### Body 2 template:
```
[Topic sentence: Another reason is that [idea].]
[Explain: [explanation].]
[Develop/Example: [detail or example].]
```

---

### Conclusion template:
```
[In conclusion, / To sum up,] I firmly believe that [restate position].
[The two main reasons for this are [brief summary of reasons].]
```

**Ví dụ:**
> "In conclusion, I firmly believe that music is one of the most effective tools for bringing people of different cultures together. The shared emotional experiences it creates and the collaborative opportunities it provides make it uniquely powerful in fostering cross-cultural understanding."

## Template 2: Discussion Essay (Discuss Both Views)

### Cấu trúc tổng thể:
```
[Introduction — paraphrase + acknowledge both views + thesis]
[Body 1 — View A]
[Body 2 — View B]
[Conclusion — restate position]
```

### Introduction template:
```
[There is considerable debate about [topic].]
[While some argue that [View A], others believe that [View B].]
[This essay will examine both perspectives before explaining why I [position].]
```

**Ví dụ (đề: "Competition vs cooperation"):**
> "There is ongoing debate about whether competition or cooperation leads to better outcomes in modern society. While some argue that a competitive environment drives individuals to reach their full potential, others contend that working together produces superior results. This essay will consider both sides of the argument before explaining why I believe a combination of the two approaches is most effective."

---

### Body 1 template (View A):
```
[Those who support [View A] argue that [main reason].]
[They point out that [explanation/evidence].]
[For example, [specific example].]
```

### Body 2 template (View B):
```
[On the other hand, those who favour [View B] believe that [main reason].]
[They point to [evidence] to support their argument.]
[For instance, [specific example].]
```

### Conclusion template:
```
[In conclusion, although both perspectives have merit,]
[I believe that [your position] is more convincing because [brief reason].]
```

## Template 3: Advantages & Disadvantages Essay

### Cấu trúc tổng thể:
```
[Introduction — paraphrase + state will discuss adv & dis]
[Body 1 — Advantages]
[Body 2 — Disadvantages]
[Conclusion — summary (+ outweigh verdict if required)]
```

### Introduction template (List):
```
[Paraphrase đề.]
[This essay will examine both the advantages and disadvantages of [topic].]
```

### Introduction template (Outweigh):
```
[Paraphrase đề.]
[This essay will argue that, on balance, the advantages/disadvantages of [topic] outweigh the disadvantages/advantages.]
```

**Ví dụ (đề: "Consumers can buy food from all over the world"):**
> "In many parts of the world, supermarkets now offer an enormous variety of foods sourced from different countries. This essay will examine both the positive and negative aspects of this development before arguing that, on balance, the benefits outweigh the drawbacks."

---

### Body 1 template (Advantages):
```
[There are several clear advantages to [topic].]
[Firstly, [main advantage], which means that [effect/explanation].]
[For example, [specific example].]
[Furthermore/In addition, [second advantage].]
```

**Ví dụ:**
> "There are several clear advantages to buying food from different countries. Firstly, consumers have access to a much wider variety of produce throughout the year, regardless of local growing seasons. For example, people in Vietnam can now enjoy fresh Norwegian salmon or French cheese that would have been unavailable a generation ago. Furthermore, global food trade often drives prices down through competition, making nutritious food more affordable for many households."

---

### Body 2 template (Disadvantages):
```
[However, there are also significant drawbacks to [topic].]
[The most serious is [main disadvantage], as [explanation].]
[Moreover, [second disadvantage], which [effect].]
```

### Conclusion template (List):
```
[In conclusion, [topic] has both clear advantages and disadvantages.]
[While [main advantage], there are also significant concerns about [main disadvantage].]
```

### Conclusion template (Outweigh — advantages win):
```
[In conclusion, although [topic] has some drawbacks, particularly [main dis],]
[I believe the advantages are greater. [Main advantage] makes [topic] a beneficial development overall.]
```

### Conclusion template (Outweigh — disadvantages win):
```
[In conclusion, while [topic] offers [main advantage],]
[the disadvantages — especially [main dis] — are too significant to ignore.]
[Overall, I believe [topic] has a negative impact on [society/environment/etc.].]
```

## Useful Phrases — Tổng hợp theo phần bài

### Mở bài (Introduction):
| Mục đích | Phrases |
|---|---|
| Paraphrase bằng passive | "It has been suggested / argued / widely claimed that..." |
| Paraphrase bằng noun phrase | "The issue of [topic] has become increasingly important in recent years." |
| Acknowledge 2 phía | "While some argue that..., others believe that..." |
| State essay purpose | "This essay will examine / explore / consider..." |
| State position | "I strongly agree/disagree with this view." / "I believe the advantages outweigh the disadvantages." |

### Thân bài (Body):
| Mục đích | Phrases |
|---|---|
| Topic sentence adv | "One of the main advantages of [topic] is that..." |
| Topic sentence dis | "However, there are also significant drawbacks to..." |
| Topic sentence view A | "Those who support [view] argue that..." |
| Topic sentence view B | "On the other hand, those who favour [view] believe that..." |
| Lý do | "This is because..." / "The reason for this is that..." |
| Kết quả | "As a result,..." / "Consequently,..." / "This means that..." |
| Ví dụ | "For example,..." / "For instance,..." / "To illustrate this,..." |
| Thêm điểm | "Furthermore,..." / "Moreover,..." / "In addition,..." |

### Kết bài (Conclusion):
| Mục đích | Phrases |
|---|---|
| Mở đầu kết luận | "In conclusion,..." / "To sum up,..." / "Overall,..." |
| Tóm tắt | "As the points above demonstrate,..." |
| Restate position | "I firmly believe that..." / "I remain convinced that..." |
| Balanced | "While both sides have merit, I believe [position] is more convincing." |
| Outweigh | "The advantages clearly outweigh the disadvantages because..." |

## Checklist hoàn chỉnh trước khi nộp bài

### Introduction:
- [ ] Paraphrase đề (không copy nguyên văn)
- [ ] Nêu rõ mình sẽ làm gì trong bài
- [ ] Có thesis / position (nếu là Opinion hoặc Outweigh)

### Body paragraphs:
- [ ] Mỗi paragraph có topic sentence rõ ràng
- [ ] Mỗi idea có giải thích / lý do
- [ ] Có ít nhất 1 ví dụ cụ thể mỗi paragraph
- [ ] Dùng linking words phù hợp (không overuse)
- [ ] Không lặp từ — dùng synonyms

### Conclusion:
- [ ] Tóm tắt ngắn gọn
- [ ] Restate position (Opinion / Outweigh)
- [ ] KHÔNG đưa ra ý mới

### General:
- [ ] Đủ 250 từ trở lên
- [ ] Style formal (không viết tắt, không bullet points)
- [ ] Kiểm tra spelling và grammar trước khi nộp
